# Scenario Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric to score analyst performance on individual offline or online scenarios.

---

## Categories

| Category | Max Points | Description |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| **Initial Access Identified** | 10 | Correctly identifies vector and evidence, or clearly explains why it is unknown based on available data. |
| **Process Analysis** | 10 | Detects malicious or suspicious execution and explains why it is unusual. |
| **Persistence Detection** | 10 | Finds tasks, services, WMI, or other persistence where present, or reasonably concludes none is visible. |
| **Credential Theft Detection** | 10 | Recognizes LSASS access, token abuse, or credential dumping behavior where applicable. |
| **Lateral Movement Analysis** | 10 | Maps movement between hosts or concludes there is no evidence of lateral movement. |
| **Timeline Accuracy** | 10 | Orders events logically with accurate timestamps and clear event relationships. |
| **Narrative Clarity** | 10 | Provides a concise, coherent description of what happened and why it matters. |
| **Remediation Recommendations** | 10 | Suggests practical steps that address root causes and detection gaps. |
| **Total** | **80** |  |

---

## Scoring Guidance

- 0–2: Not demonstrated or largely incorrect.  
- 3–5: Partially correct, missing key elements or depth.  
- 6–8: Mostly correct with minor gaps.  
- 9–10: Complete, accurate, and clearly articulated.
